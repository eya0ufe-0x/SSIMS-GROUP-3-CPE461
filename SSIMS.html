About SSIMS

Welcome to SSIMS

SSIMS is dedicated to bridging the gap between Computer Engineering students and industry partners. We streamline the SIWES and internship placement process, making it easier for students to find meaningful opportunities and for companies to discover talented engineers.
Our mission is to create a seamless platform that connects academic excellence with real-world experience, fostering growth for both students and organizations.
